Built for the Future of Commerce: Our Technology

BeaverOS is designed with a modern **microservices architecture** for robustness, independence, and scalability. We leverage cutting-edge technologies:

  • Backend: **Python with FastAPI** (efficient and rapid development)
  • POS Desktop: **C++ with Qt** (high performance and native user experience)
  • Admin Web Panel: **React/Next.js** (modern and reactive interface)
  • Database: **PostgreSQL** (reliability and power)
  • Containers: **Docker** (consistent and easy deployment)
